Trips

Rapids Rafting offers a variety of trips for all skill levels. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced rafter, we have the perfect trip for you. Our experienced guides will take you on an adventure you will never forget.

We offer half-day, full-day, and multi-day trips. Our trips include all the necessary equipment, including life jackets, helmets, and paddles. We also provide transportation to and from the river.

Our trips are suitable for individuals, families, and groups. We can accommodate groups of up to 50 people. We also offer private trips for those who want a more personalized experience.

If you have any questions or would like to book a trip, please contact us. We look forward to seeing you on the river!

Our Trips


Trip 1

Trip 1 is an ideal half-day adventure for beginners eager to experience rafting. This carefully designed excursion guides you through serene waters and gentle rapids, allowing participants to become familiar with the basics of rafting without overwhelming them. The calm stretches of water offer a perfect opportunity to learn paddling techniques, while the mild rapids provide just enough excitement to make the trip enjoyable. As you float down the river, you can take in the beautiful surroundings and appreciate the peaceful environment. Trip 1 is perfect for families, friends, or individuals looking to try something new and adventurous. Experienced guides are on hand to ensure safety and provide valuable tips, making the journey educational and fun. The brief duration of the trip makes it a convenient option for those with limited time, yet it still offers a memorable outdoor experience. Whether you're looking to dip your toes into the world of rafting or simply enjoy a relaxing day on the water, Trip 1 is the perfect choice.

Trip 2 is a full-day adventure tailored for experienced rafters eager to challenge themselves. This exhilarating journey navigates through demanding rapids and turbulent waters, providing the perfect opportunity to test your skills and push your limits. The trip offers a continuous stream of excitement as you encounter powerful currents and navigate through rough waters. Ideal for thrill-seekers, Trip 2 combines the thrill of conquering difficult rapids with the satisfaction of honing your rafting techniques. The stunning landscapes and dynamic river conditions make this a visually captivating and physically demanding experience. Expert guides accompany you, offering insights and ensuring your safety throughout the journey. With their guidance, you can refine your skills and tackle even the most daunting sections of the river. Trip 2 is perfect for those looking to immerse themselves in a full day of adventure and challenge. Gather your fellow experienced rafters and prepare for an unforgettable journey through some of the most exciting and rough waters available.

Trip 3

Embark on an unforgettable expedition with Trip 3, ideal for groups seeking adventure. This thrilling journey navigates through challenging rapids and turbulent waters, providing an exhilarating experience. Each day offers new excitement as you conquer the powerful currents and navigate the rough waters. The camaraderie built while paddling together enhances the trip, making it perfect for friends or colleagues looking for a bonding experience. The stunning scenery and the adrenaline rush of the rapids create memories that last a lifetime. Expert guides ensure safety and provide insights into the natural surroundings, enriching the overall experience. This multi-day adventure offers a perfect blend of thrill and relaxation, with evenings spent around the campfire sharing stories and laughter. Whether you are a seasoned rafter or a beginner, Trip 3 promises excitement and fun for everyone. So, gather your group and prepare for an amazing rafting journey through some of the most exciting and rough waters you'll ever encounter.
